Data Deletion Request

Last updated: November 1, 2025

At My Vote, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal information. If you wish to delete your account or any data associated with your use of the My Vote app, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data in one of the following ways:

  • Send an email to sivakumar.annadurai@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request - My Vote".
  • Include your registered email address (used for Google or Facebook login) in the message so we can identify your account.

What Happens Next

Once we receive your request, we will:

  • Verify your identity via your registered login email;
  • Delete all associated personal data from our systems within 7 working days;
  • Send you a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Data That Cannot Be Deleted

We do not store or modify any public election data sourced from the Election Commission of India. As such, this public data cannot be altered or deleted by us.
